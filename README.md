# Tea-Production-Market-Analysis-Dashboard

I recently worked on an end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project focused on understanding the dynamics of tea production, pricing, sales, and profitability across regions and tea types (Darjeeling vs Tamil Nadu).

Using Python libraries like Pandas, Seaborn, and Matplotlib, I transformed raw data into meaningful insights through multiple visualizations and comparative analysis.

🔍 Project Objectives & Analysis Performed:

• Export Analysis (Country-wise)
Compared tea export volumes across major countries to identify international demand patterns and regional preferences.

• Sales Analysis (State-wise)
Analyzed how different states contribute to overall sales, highlighting strong and weak markets for each tea type.

• Production vs Price Relationship
Explored how production levels influence pricing, uncovering an inverse relationship where higher production often correlates with lower prices.

• Price Trend Over Time
Tracked yearly price variations to understand market stability, fluctuations, and long-term trends in tea pricing.

• Business Insights (Profit & Revenue)
Evaluated profitability and revenue generation across tea types, revealing which segment performs better financially.

📈 Key Insights:

• Tamil Nadu tea dominates in production and export volume
• Darjeeling tea commands significantly higher prices (premium segment)
• Higher production tends to reduce price due to supply dynamics
• Darjeeling tea generates higher profit margins despite lower volume
• Tamil Nadu tea drives consistent revenue through scale

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used:

• Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
• Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
• Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
• Data Visualization

💡 This project helped me better understand how data can reveal market behavior, pricing strategies, and business performance in a simple yet powerful way.
